## Taipan fonts

Taipan uses two fonts, TaipanStandardFont and TaipanThickFont

TaipanStandardFont is used for almost all text in the game.

TaipanThickFont is used for the Firm name, and also has glyphs used for drawing boxes.
It also has glyphs for showing enemy ships, though we may simply use large sprites for these.

## Drawing boxes with fonts

These glyphs from TaipanThickFont can be used to make boxes.

129: Vertical bar
136: Bottom left corner and top left corner (bottom of one box, and top of box underneath)
137: Bottom right corner and top right corner (bottom of one box, and top of box underneath)
141: Horizontal line for top of box
154: Horizontal line for bottom of box
156: Bottom left corner
157: Two horizontal lines, for bottom of one box, and top of box underneath (to combine with 136 and 137)
158: Bottom right corner
187: Top left corner
189: Top right corner
